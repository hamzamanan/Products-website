Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

We have been established for over 20 years, we provide high quality tailor-made metal fabrications usually for domestic use in Yorkshire, we also consistently work with companies. Our workers are qualified & and have more than 10 years experience. We operate quick and have an efficient turn over time. Our detailed work is protected by our one year guarantee.

Sheikh Fabrication will keep your custom metal manufacturing project to your liking from pre-fabrication to finishing and distribution, removing the need for subcontracting work to other businesses, putting a strain on your budget and deadlines.

Projects to produce metal made by Sheikh Fabrication are proudly made in the UK. Our team is able to create, develop, install, inspect, disassemble, and deliver your project to its venue. Because of the investment in technology and in our professional workforce of committed manufacturers, Sheikh Fabrication is able to provide a wide range of metal fabrication services.
